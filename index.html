CHEERLEADING

ABOUT

Cheerleading is a sport that performs for sports team motivation,it consists of sideline cheer and competition cheer. Sidleine Cheer is mostly the chanting and arm motions with a little bit of dancing combined with everything else. Competiton Cheer is a routine that ranges from one to three minutes,which contains tumbling, dance, jumps, cheers, and stunting. A Cheerleader role is to build a sense of community and school spirit both on and off the field and court. You have to keep your appearance up and make sure that you are approachable, since you are a cheerleader. Always conduct yourself in a manner that brings spirit to the team.
